My Toolset

Here are the technologies that I most use. They are maintained by very respectible teams. I am sure my development expirence would be totally different without these tools, as they are very powerful and flexable at the same time. You can see most of them at work right in from of your eyes now.

JavaScript Framework

vue.js and NUXT.js

And I thought you could so a lot with JavaScript. Once I dove into the vue.js world it was a game changer for me. I have tinkered around with other JS frameworks, but this one really stuck with me.

CSS Framework

Tailwind CSS

Tailwind is awesome in many ways. It is like a supercharged CSS, or a powerfull inline framework that really speeds up my workflow. It introduced me to concepts that were new to me and really gave me the drive to be up-to-date on new web tech.

Vector Manipulation

Inkscape

Before I found out how great Inkscape was, I felt very alone in a world without Adobe. Inkscape made everything ok again, and, actually it was much better.

Operating System

Linux

I would never have made it this far without ya buddy! Linux in all it's flavors, it is hard to not admit it comes very close to perfection in one form or another. If you have not yet tried a full Linux distro, it will be the best thing you have ever done for your development carrer.
